Use of Time in Peru (2010-2024) and UK (2024)

An in-depth analysis of time use patterns across this two countries and years, examining how people allocate their time to different activities and how this has changed over time. This is an important issue because the use of time is one of the latest problems that people are facing after the pandemic. The effects and changes from years before and now are relevant in terms of policy making and proposing new public interventions.
